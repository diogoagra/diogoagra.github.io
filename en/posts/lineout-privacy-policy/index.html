Privacy Policy - Line Out

1. Introduction

the game Line Out is provided by Diogo Agra (“Developer”). This Privacy Policy describes how the game handles user information.

2. Data Collection

Line Out does not collect, store, or transmit any type of personal, sensitive, or technical user information. The game works entirely offline and locally, does not monitor activities, and does not request permissions to access private data.

3. Ads and Payments

  • No Ads: Line Out does not display third-party advertisements.
  • No Payments: The game has no in-app purchases and does not require any form of payment or subscription.

4. Information Sharing

Since no data is collected, there is no sharing of information with third parties, partners, or advertising networks.

5. Security

The game is designed to ensure the integrity of your device, not performing operations that could compromise the security of your local files.

6. Changes to this Policy

This Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Policy, contact us via email: contato@diogoagra.dev.
